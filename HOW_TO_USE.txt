   - AWS Access Key ID: (from your .env file)
   - AWS Secret Access Key: (from your .env file)

   - Click "Choose File" and select an MP4 video
   - Click "Upload to S3"

   - Enter captions in format: start,end,text
   - Example:
     0.72,3.84,Big man in a suit of armor
     3.84,6.72,Take that off. What are you?
   - Choose caption style (bottom/top/karaoke)
   - Click "Render Video"

5. Wait for completion:
   - Status updates automatically every 5 seconds
   - Download button appears when ready (~60 seconds)
